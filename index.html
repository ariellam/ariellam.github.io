Ariel Lam

Developer, hacker, designer, artist.



Hey! I'm Ariel. I'm a Software Engineering student at the Unversity of Waterloo. I'm interested in mobile UI/UX design and development, making cool things, and combining my creativity with code. On my spare time, I love to read, listen to indie rock, and draw.

Projects

Sweather

Sweather is an upcoming app which gives you outfit recommendations based on previous days with similar climate conditions.

Created using Adobe Illustrator, Sketch and Principle.



EduMatch

Github

EduMatch is a social platform that allows high school and university students to connect with Mentors – industry professionals, business gurus, experienced scholars – who can give them advice for post-secondary education and beyond.

Designed in Adobe Photoshop and prototyped in Adobe Experience Design.

Won Best Use of Adobe Creative Cloud at EduHacks 2017, Vancouver, BC.



GivingTree

Devpost Github

GivingTree allows users to keep track of what they are lending to people and to whom they owe something. Each user takes care of their own "karma" tree which grows or withers depending on the positive or negative transactions with others.

Developed using Android Studio, graphics designed in Adobe Photoshop.

Won First Place at The Lady Hacks 2016 at York University.



Lucky Last Press

Website

Lucky Last Press is an online store and design brand, dedicating to making cool accessories and pins.

After collecting pins and patches for a little over a year, I decided to combine my design and art experience into a business venture. Thus, LLP was born!
